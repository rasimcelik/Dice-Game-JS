Welcome To Dice Game

Player one always starts first
Both players will use the same button, "Roll Dice," when their turn comes
To win the game, you must roll the dice until you do not roll a "1."
If you roll a "1," your turn ends automatically, and it becomes your opponent's turn
Before rolling a "1," you have the option to "Hold" the dice. If you choose to hold, all the dice you rolled before will be added to your score
Each player has a current score and a total score. The current score represents the dice rolls in the current turn, while the total score accumulates all the held scores
The game continues until one player reaches a total score of 100 or more
When a player's total score reaches 100 or more, they win the game
The game can be restarted by clicking the "New Game" button
